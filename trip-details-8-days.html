The Experience

This 8-day safari invites you to explore Tanzania's most spectacular wildlife destinations. From the bustling gateway town of Arusha to the baobab-dotted plains of Tarangire, every stop is filled with adventure and discovery. The Serengeti takes center stage, offering the chance to witness the legendary Great Migration. The journey ends at the Ngorongoro Crater, home to an incredible variety of wildlife in a breathtaking natural amphitheater.
