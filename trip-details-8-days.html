8-Day Tanzania Great Migration Safari

8 Days / 7 Nights
Tarangire & Serengeti
Migration & Ngorongoro
Private Tour

Trip Overview

This 8-day safari invites you to explore Tanzania's most spectacular wildlife destinations. From the bustling gateway town of Arusha to the baobab-dotted plains of Tarangire, every stop is filled with adventure and discovery. The Serengeti takes center stage, offering the chance to witness the legendary Great Migration. The journey ends at the Ngorongoro Crater, home to an incredible variety of wildlife in a breathtaking natural amphitheater. With comfortable standard lodges and camps, this safari balances excitement and relaxation perfectly.

Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Arusha

When you land at Kilimanjaro International Airport, your driver-guide will be waiting with a warm welcome. A short transfer takes you to your lodge in Arusha, where you can relax after your flight and soak in the fresh mountain air. The town sits at the foot of Mount Meru and serves as the ideal starting point for safaris. Spend your first evening enjoying dinner and resting before the adventure begins. Tomorrow, the wild landscapes of Tanzania await you.

Accommodation: Standard Arusha Hotel / Airport Lodge
Meal Plan: Half Board
Day 2

Tarangire National Park

After breakfast, you head out to Tarangire National Park, known for its giant baobab trees and huge herds of elephants. The park is alive with wildlife, from zebras and giraffes to predators such as lions and leopards. The Tarangire River flows through the park and draws animals year-round, making it a great spot for game viewing. You will spend the day exploring these beautiful landscapes, with picnic lunch in the wild. In the evening, settle at your lodge for dinner and a quiet night in nature.

Accommodation: Sangawire Tented Lodge / Maramboi Tented Lodge / Tarangire Ndovu Tented Lodge
Meal Plan: Full Board
Day 3

Tarangire Game Drive & Walking Safari

This morning, enjoy a guided walking safari for a closer look at Tarangire's plants, tracks, and smaller creatures. Walking through the bush gives you a new perspective, different from the safari vehicle. After lunch, continue with an afternoon game drive to see elephants under baobabs and herds grazing across the plains. The golden light of evening makes this drive especially beautiful. Return to your lodge for dinner and stories around the fire.

Accommodation: Sangawire Tented Lodge / Maramboi Tented Lodge / Tarangire Ndovu Tented Lodge
Meal Plan: Full Board
Day 4

Journey to the Serengeti

After breakfast, begin the scenic drive towards the Serengeti, passing through the rolling hills of the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. As you enter the Serengeti, the endless plains open up before you, home to incredible numbers of wildlife. Keep your eyes open for giraffes, antelopes, and predators along the way. By afternoon, you'll reach your camp inside the park. After dinner, fall asleep to the distant roars of lions and the sounds of the wilderness.

Accommodation: Zawadi Serengeti Camp / Malaika Seronera Camp / Nyota Luxury Camp
Meal Plan: Full Board
Day 5

Serengeti Safari - Following the Migration

Spend the whole day exploring the Serengeti, focusing on the spectacular Great Migration if you're traveling in season. Depending on the time of year, you may see vast herds of wildebeest crossing rivers or calving in the southern plains. The drama of predator and prey is ever-present, with cheetahs, lions, and leopards always nearby. A picnic lunch keeps you in the middle of the action throughout the day. As evening falls, return to camp for dinner and rest.

Accommodation: Zawadi Serengeti Camp / Malaika Seronera Camp / Nyota Luxury Camp
Meal Plan: Full Board
Day 6

Serengeti Game Drive or Balloon Safari

At sunrise, you may choose an optional hot air balloon safari to see the plains from above, or enjoy a traditional morning game drive. Either way, the Serengeti's beauty will take your breath away as the animals begin their day. After lunch, continue exploring different areas of the park, seeking out elephants, buffalo, and large prides of lions. Each drive reveals new surprises in this vast wilderness. Back at camp, enjoy dinner under the African night sky.

Accommodation: Zawadi Serengeti Camp / Malaika Seronera Camp / Nyota Luxury Camp
Meal Plan: Full Board
Day 7

Ngorongoro Conservation Area

Begin your day with a final Serengeti game drive before heading towards the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. The drive offers changing scenery, from wide plains to lush highlands filled with Maasai villages. Arrive at your lodge near the crater rim in the afternoon, where the views are truly unforgettable. Spend the evening relaxing and enjoying a hearty dinner. Rest well, as tomorrow you descend into one of the world's most remarkable wildlife sanctuaries.

Accommodation: Ngorongoro Marera Mt. View Lodge / Acacia Farm Lodge / Ngorongoro Oldean Lodge
Meal Plan: Full Board
Day 8

Ngorongoro Crater & Departure

Today you descend into the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site often called Africa's "Garden of Eden." The crater floor is home to lions, zebras, wildebeests, buffalo, and even the endangered black rhino. Enjoy a picnic lunch by a hippo pool as you take in the breathtaking scenery. After your game drive, ascend and drive back towards Arusha. In the late afternoon, transfer to Kilimanjaro Airport for your onward flight or continue with a Zanzibar beach extension.
