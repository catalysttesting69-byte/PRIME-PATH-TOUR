The Experience

This 10-day adventure takes you deep into Tanzania's most celebrated parks, from the sweeping Serengeti plains to the lush forests of Ngorongoro and the elephant havens of Tarangire. Designed for wildlife lovers, this journey gives you the chance to spot the Big Five and countless other species. Beyond wildlife, you'll also experience vibrant culture during your village visit at Mto wa Mbu.
