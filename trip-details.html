Trip Overview

Discover the best of Zanzibar in this 6-day tour combining culture, nature, and relaxation. Explore the historic streets of Stone Town, visit spice farms, and relax on beautiful beaches. Enjoy snorkeling at Mnemba Atoll, swim with turtles, and experience the island's vibrant local life. Perfect for those looking to explore, unwind, and enjoy the unique charm of the Spice Islands.

Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Zanzibar & Forodhani Gardens Night Market

Welcome to Zanzibar! Upon arrival at Abeid Amani Karume International Airport, our professional driver will greet you and escort you to your hotel for check-in and relaxation. In the evening, immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of the Forodhani Gardens Night Market. Savor authentic Zanzibari street food like Zanzibar pizza, seafood skewers, and sugarcane juice while enjoying the lively local atmosphere.

Accommodation: Antonio Garden Hotel / Marumaru Hotel / Serena Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 2

Spice Farm Tour, Stone Town Exploration & Nakupenda Sandbanks

After breakfast, head to a local spice farm for a guided tour where you'll discover the origins of Zanzibar's nickname: the "Spice Island." Engage your senses with cloves, vanilla, nutmeg, and other exotic spices. Continue to historic Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, where you'll explore winding alleys, ancient doors, and local markets. In the afternoon, take a boat ride to Nakupenda Sandbank for a relaxing beach escape with crystal-clear waters and snorkeling.

Accommodation: Antonio Garden Hotel / Marumaru Hotel / Serena Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 3

Jozani Forest, Kuza Cave & Blue Lagoon

Today begins with a guided walk through Jozani Forest, home to the rare Red Colobus Monkeys and rich tropical flora. Afterward, visit Kuza Cave, a hidden gem where you can swim in its clear, mineral-rich spring water and learn about Swahili culture at the small cultural center. Conclude your day at Blue Lagoon for a serene snorkeling experience among colorful corals and marine life.

Accommodation: Antonio Garden Hotel / Marumaru Hotel / Serena Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 4

Mnemba Atoll, Turtle Swimming & Sunset Dhow Cruise

Set out early for a full-day excursion to Mnemba Atoll, Zanzibar's premier snorkeling destination, where you'll swim in turquoise waters teeming with marine life. Later, visit the Nungwi Natural Aquarium to swim with and feed sea turtles in a safe, ethical environment. In the evening, unwind with a traditional dhow cruise as the sun sets over the Indian Ocean, accompanied by soft Swahili music and light refreshments.

Accommodation: Sunset Kendwa Hotel / Kendwa Rocks Hotel / Gold Zanzibar Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 5

Leisure Time & Cultural Village Tour

Spend your morning at leisure—relax by the beach, enjoy your resort's amenities, or book an optional massage or water sport. In the afternoon, embark on a cultural village tour where you'll interact with locals, learn about traditional crafts, and gain deeper insights into everyday rural life in Zanzibar. This is a unique opportunity to experience the authentic soul of the island.

Accommodation: Sunset Kendwa Hotel / Kendwa Rocks Hotel / Gold Zanzibar Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 6

Departure & Last-Minute Stone Town Shopping

After breakfast and hotel check-out, enjoy some free time for last-minute shopping in Stone Town's lively bazaars. Pick up souvenirs such as spices, handcrafted jewelry, fabrics, or wood carvings. Our driver will then transfer you to the airport for your departure flight, marking the end of your Zanzibar adventure with memories to last a lifetime.
