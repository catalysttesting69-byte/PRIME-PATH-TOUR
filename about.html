Our Heritage

A Shared Vision of Authenticity

Prime Path Tours and Safaris was founded in 2025 by Tanzanian entrepreneurs Mariam Kusaga and her husband Riam Urassa. Their shared vision was simple yet powerful: to create a travel company rooted in deep local expertise and genuine professionalism.

Specializing in tailor-made private safaris and curated group adventures, we bridge the gap between travelers and the true spirit of Tanzania. Each journey we craft is led by experienced professionals who speak the language of the land and the heart.

Our Mission

To design personalized and immersive travel experiences that celebrate Africa's beauty, culture, and wildlife. We are committed to responsible tourism and creating memories that last a lifetime for every traveler we serve.

Our Vision

To become a trusted gateway to unforgettable journeys across Africa, inspiring a deep connection between travelers and the natural and cultural wonders of the continent.
