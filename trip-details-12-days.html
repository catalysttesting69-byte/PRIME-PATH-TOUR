Safari & Zanzibar

12 Days Tanzania Safari & Zanzibar Beach Holiday Escape

12 Days / 11 Nights
Serengeti & Zanzibar
Romantic Honeymoon
Private Tour

Trip Overview

This romantic 12-day journey is designed for couples seeking both adventure and relaxation. Your safari begins in Tarangire, Lake Manyara, Serengeti, and Ngorongoro—parks teeming with wildlife and breathtaking beauty. Days are filled with thrilling game drives, guided walks, and encounters with the Big Five. After the safari, fly to Zanzibar to unwind on its white-sand beaches and explore the cultural charm of Stone Town. With luxurious lodges, tented camps, and beachfront resorts, this itinerary blends wild adventure with pure serenity.

Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Arusha – A Warm Beginning

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport where your guide will greet you with a smile and warm Tanzanian hospitality. A scenic drive takes you to your lodge in Arusha, surrounded by lush gardens and views of Mount Meru. Take the evening to rest and enjoy the peaceful environment. This day is all about relaxation and preparation for the days ahead. Overnight stay in Arusha offers you the perfect soft start to your journey.

Accommodation: Pazur Inn Arusha / Forest Hill Hotel / Grand Melia Arusha
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 2

Tarangire National Park – Land of Giants

Your safari adventure begins with a drive to Tarangire, a park known for its towering baobabs and impressive elephant herds. As you enter, expect to see zebras, wildebeests, giraffes, and perhaps even a lion lounging in the shade. A game drive along the Tarangire River reveals the park's vibrant ecosystem. In the evening, relax at your tented lodge, enjoying dinner under a starlit African sky. The wilderness sounds create a truly magical backdrop for your first safari night.

Accommodation: Sangaiwe Tented Lodge / Maramboi Tented Lodge / Tarangire Ndovu Tented Lodge
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Day 3

Lake Manyara National Park – Beauty and Contrast

After breakfast, continue to Lake Manyara, famous for its pink flamingos and tree-climbing lions. The park is a delightful mix of forests, grasslands, and wetlands, making it a haven for birds and diverse wildlife. You'll enjoy a game drive with chances to see elephants, buffalo, and hippos by the shimmering lake. Afterward, check into your lodge overlooking the Great Rift Valley for sweeping views. As the day ends, relax and savor the tranquil atmosphere of this unique destination.

Accommodation: Sangaiwe Tented Lodge / Maramboi Tented Lodge / Tarangire Ndovu Tented Lodge
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Day 4

Journey to Serengeti – Endless Plains

Today, travel through the Ngorongoro highlands before descending onto the legendary Serengeti plains. Along the way, you may spot Maasai herders with their cattle, adding a cultural touch to the journey. An afternoon game drive introduces you to the Serengeti's incredible wildlife, from grazing antelopes to prowling predators. As dusk falls, settle into your luxury tented camp in the heart of the savannah. Surrounded by nature, enjoy dinner while listening to the sounds of the wild.

Accommodation: Zawadi Serengeti Camp / Malaika Seronera Camp / Nyota Luxury Camp
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Day 5

Full-Day Safari in Serengeti – The Wild Heart of Africa

Rise early to catch the golden sunrise, the perfect moment to begin a full-day game drive. Traverse the vast plains in search of lions, leopards, cheetahs, and the great herds of wildebeest and zebras. Stop for a picnic lunch in the wilderness, taking in the panoramic beauty around you. The Serengeti offers countless moments of wonder and photography opportunities. End the day with dinner and stories around the campfire at your lodge.

Accommodation: Zawadi Serengeti Camp / Malaika Seronera Camp / Nyota Luxury Camp
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Day 6

Ngorongoro Crater – Nature's Masterpiece

After breakfast, bid farewell to Serengeti and make your way to Ngorongoro, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Descend into the crater for an unforgettable safari within its lush and diverse habitats. Here, you may spot rhinos grazing, lions stalking, and flamingos gathering at the lake. Pause for a picnic beside a hippo pool, surrounded by extraordinary scenery. Return to the crater rim in the evening for dinner with breathtaking views.

Accommodation: Farm of Dreams Lodge / Acacia Farm Lodge / Ngorongoro Serena Lodge
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Day 7

Fly to Zanzibar – The Spice Island Awaits

This morning, board a flight to Zanzibar, where your beach adventure takes a romantic turn. Upon arrival, transfer to your elegant hotel in Stone Town, the island's historic heart. Spend the afternoon exploring narrow streets filled with markets, Swahili architecture, and vibrant history. As evening falls, enjoy ocean views. The day offers a seamless transition from wild safari to coastal charm.

Accommodation: Spice Palace Hotel / Tembo Hotel / Serena Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 8

Spice Farm, Prison Island & Stone Town Tour

After breakfast, head to a traditional spice plantation to discover Zanzibar's rich history of spices. Continue to Prison Island for giant tortoises and snorkeling opportunities. In the afternoon, explore the narrow alleys of Stone Town, filled with culture, architecture, and lively markets.

Accommodation: Spice Palace Hotel / Tembo Hotel / Serena Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 9

Safari Blue Experience

Embark on a full-day Safari Blue excursion, sailing on traditional dhows. Discover sandbanks, snorkel in coral reefs, and enjoy a seafood lunch on a private island. The turquoise waters and dolphin sightings make this one of Zanzibar's most unforgettable experiences. Return for a relaxed evening.

Accommodation: Spice Palace Hotel / Tembo Hotel / Serena Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 10

Leisure in Zanzibar

A day at leisure to soak in the beauty of Zanzibar's beaches. Relax by the pool or indulge in optional activities such as snorkeling, diving, or visiting nearby villages. This day is designed for unwinding and personal exploration at your own pace. Overnight stay at the resort.

Accommodation: Spice Palace Hotel / Tembo Hotel / Serena Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 11

Mnemba Atoll Snorkeling, Turtle Aquarium & Sunset Dhow Cruise

Get ready for an early trip to Mnemba Atoll, one of the top snorkeling spots in Zanzibar. Swim in clear waters and discover colorful fish and corals. There's also a chance to see turtles in their natural habitat. After snorkeling, relax on the nearby beach or return to your hotel for downtime. A laid-back evening to enjoy the beach vibes.

Accommodation: Spice Palace Hotel / Tembo Hotel / Serena Hotel
Meal Plan: Bed & Breakfast
Day 12

Departure – Farewell Tanzania & Zanzibar

After a relaxed breakfast overlooking the turquoise ocean, prepare for your onward journey. Depending on your flight time, you may enjoy a final swim or a stroll along the shore. Your driver will transfer you to Zanzibar International Airport for your departure. Though your honeymoon ends, the memories of safaris, sunsets, and seaside serenity will remain with you forever. Tanzania and Zanzibar will always hold a place in your hearts.
